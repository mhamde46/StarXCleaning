# **App Name**: Star X Cleaning

## Core Features:

- Service Display: Showcase all cleaning services with clear descriptions and high-quality icons (home, office, carpet, deep cleaning, end-of-tenancy, etc.). Include sample pricing (e.g., “From £20/hour”) and a “Request a Custom Quote” button. Use expandable service cards or hover effects for detailed info (duration, cleaning materials, guarantees).
- Booking System: Integrated booking form allowing clients to select: Service type, property size, preferred date/time, and contact details. Include WhatsApp integration for instant booking or support (+44 9169 87846). Optional: connect to Google Calendar for real-time scheduling. Automatic confirmation message & follow-up email.
- Testimonial Carousel: Rotating slider with client reviews (star ratings + photos). Include Before/After photo slider to visually show results. Optional: auto-import Google Reviews for authenticity.
- Contact & Support: Dedicated Contact section with: Interactive contact form. Displayed company info: STAR X CLEANING LTD Bradford, West Yorkshire, United Kingdom Email: info@starxcleaning.co.uk Phone: +44 9169 87846. Add clickable buttons for Call Now, Email, and WhatsApp Chat. Embedded Google Map for service area visualization.
- AI-Powered FAQ Generation: Implement an AI tool (or backend script) that scans the website’s content to: Generate a dynamic FAQ section automatically, Cover common queries (pricing, availability, cleaning products, cancellation policy, etc.), Update automatically as new content or services are added. This improves user experience and reduces manual support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary: Blue (#007BFF) — trust & professionalism.
- Accent: Green (#2ECC71) — eco-friendly and refreshing.
- Neutral: White (#FFFFFF) and Light Grey (#F6F8FA) — clean and modern.
- Minimalist structure with clean grid sections. Rounded corners and soft drop shadows for a friendly and modern look. Sticky navigation bar with smooth scroll to sections. Clear CTA buttons in bright green or blue (“Book Now”, “Get a Quote”).
- Headlines: ‘Playfair Display’, serif — elegant and trustworthy. Body Text: ‘PT Sans’, sans-serif — clean and highly readable. Maintain a balance between professionalism and warmth.
- Use animated vector icons (Lottie or SVG) representing: Mop, broom, spray bottle, house, office, leaf (eco-friendly). Soft color transitions and motion on hover for engagement.
- Smooth fade-ins on scroll. Subtle parallax backgrounds for hero and testimonial sections. Micro-interactions on buttons and icons (hover & click feedback).
